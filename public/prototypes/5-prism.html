FAQ

What is Clawbster?

Clawbster is an AI builder collective where members meet in real life to share their agentic AI journeys, demonstrate what they build with AI agents, and help each other level up. With 12+ meetups across 4 cities and 50+ builders, it's one of the most active in-person AI builder communities.

How do I join?

Sign up through our Luma page. Meetups are free and open to anyone building with AI agents and agentic workflows. No pitch decks needed — just bring something you built, something you broke, or something you can't figure out alone.

What happens at a meetup?

Members sit at tables, show their screens, and share what actually works. Every meetup is a working session where real knowledge transfers happen — members bring agentic workflows, automation pipelines, and hard-won lessons from deploying AI.
