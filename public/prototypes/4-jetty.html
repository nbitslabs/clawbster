A collective of builders who meet in real life. We are not a Discord server. We are not a Slack channel. We are people who believe the most important conversations about AI happen face-to-face, around tables, with screens open and ideas flowing freely between minds that refuse to build alone.

We sit at tables, show our screens, and share what actually works. No pitch decks. No networking cards. Just builders who show up with something they made, something they broke, or something they cannot figure out alone. Every meetup is a working session where real knowledge transfers happen.

We teach each other what works with AI. The gap between reading about agents and building them is enormous. We close that gap together. Members bring their agentic workflows, their automation pipelines, their hard-won lessons from deploying AI in production, and we all leave sharper than we arrived.
