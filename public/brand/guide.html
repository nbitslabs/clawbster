Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use Fraunces for all headings and the logo
  • Use Inter for body text, buttons, and UI
  • Keep the warm parchment background (#FAF8F5)
  • Use Claw Red as the primary accent
  • Give the mascot room to breathe
  • Maintain transparent background on mascot
  • Use drop-shadow (not box-shadow) on mascot

Don't

  • Don't stretch or distort the mascot
  • Don't place mascot on busy backgrounds
  • Don't use pure white (#FFF) as page background
  • Don't mix in other serif fonts
  • Don't use Claw Red for large text blocks
  • Don't add borders or outlines to the mascot
